Principal's Introduction

The principal, Md. Abdul Ahed, is dedicated to advancing the school’s mission by emphasizing academic excellence and holistic student development.

Institutional Mission

Narsingdi Government Girls High School is committed to nurturing young minds through a curriculum that fosters intellectual curiosity, ethical responsibility, and a deep-rooted sense of community service. The institution aspires to create a supportive learning environment where students are empowered to thrive and make meaningful contributions to society.

Admission Guidelines

Eligibility: Students who have successfully completed primary education.
Application Procedure: Available at the school office or accessible online.
Selection Method: Based on academic merit and entrance assessments.

Educational Framework

The academic program is aligned with the national curriculum, offering a comprehensive education across the Sciences, Humanities, and Commerce. The school is committed to fostering critical thinking and a lifelong love of learning.

Extracurricular and Co-Curricular Programs

  • Cultural Events: Including drama, music, and traditional dance.
  • Athletics: A diverse range of sports, including football and basketball.
  • Oratory and Debate: Opportunities for public speaking and debate.
  • Science and Technology Club: Hands-on experiments and research projects.
  • Literary Activities: Engaging in writing and literary competitions.
  • Eco-Initiatives: Programs focused on environmental stewardship.
  • Art and Creativity: Workshops in various creative disciplines.
  • Community Outreach: Projects aimed at social responsibility.

Available Resources

  • Comprehensive Library: Offering a rich collection of educational materials.
  • Laboratories: Fully equipped for hands-on learning in sciences and IT.
  • Sports Facilities: Expansive playgrounds for physical education.
